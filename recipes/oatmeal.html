Oatmeal Recipe

The finished dish - Oatmeal with fruits!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of quick cook Steel Cut Oats
  • 2.5 cups of water
  • 1 cup of frozen wild blueberries
  • 1 cup of frozen pomegranate
  • 1 sliced banana

Steps

  1. Bring 2.5 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add 1 cup of quick cook Steel cut oats, stir, and then lower the heat to medium-low.
  3. Add in the sliced banana.
  4. Let cook uncovered for 5 minutes -or until desired texture is reached-. Stir occasionally.
  5. Pour into a bowl and add the frozen fruit.
