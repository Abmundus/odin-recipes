Chickpeas Recipe

The finished dish - Boiled Chickpeas!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 cup of dry chickpeas
  • 1/2 tsp of baking soda x2

Steps

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight in water and add 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  2. Use a strainer to get rid of the liquid and rinse the chickpeas.
  3. Add the chickpeas, 6 cups of water, another 1/2 tsp of baking soda to a pot.
  4. Bring to a boil.
  5. Stir, get rid of any resulting foam, reduce the heat to a simmer.
  6. Cover the pot and let cook for 45 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat, use the strainer to dispose of the cooking liquid.
  8. Enjoy your chickpeas! :)
