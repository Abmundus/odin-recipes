Rice Recipe

The finished dish - Boiled Rice!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sushi rice
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Place 1/2 of sushi rice in a mesh strainer under running water until the resulting liquid is clear.
  2. In a pot, bring 1 cup of water to a boil.
  3. Add the rice, stir, and then lower heat to low.
  4. Cover the pot, and let cook for 18 minutes.
  5. Let stand for 5 minutes -while keeping the pot covered-.
  6. Fluff with a fork and serve.
